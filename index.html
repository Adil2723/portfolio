✦ About

Engineering across every layer of the stack.

I'm a second-year BSCS student at FAST-NUCES Karachi with a strong interest in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Science, and Software Development. I enjoy building technology that solves real-world problems through intelligent systems and practical software solutions.

My experience spans x86 Assembly, C/C++ systems programming, full-stack web development, and AI applications built with Python, PyTorch, and OpenCV. I've developed projects including a face-recognition attendance system, a GUI-based chess game, a full-stack cinema booking platform, a restaurant management system, and a multithreaded OS simulator that models real-world concurrency using POSIX threads, semaphores, mutexes, and condition variables.

Beyond academics, I serve as Deputy Head of Competitive Programming for PROCOM '26 and Co-Head of the Digital Scavenger Hunt for DevDay '26, where I help organize technical competitions and events that engage hundreds of students.

I'm currently expanding my skills in Machine Learning, Computer Vision, and mobile application development with Flutter, while actively seeking opportunities to apply AI and software engineering to meaningful, real-world challenges.
